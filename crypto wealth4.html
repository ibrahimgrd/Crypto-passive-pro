Top Crypto Passive Income Methods

Staking

Lock your crypto to support blockchain operations and earn rewards—often with attractive APYs. Popular for coins like ETH, ADA, SOL.

  • Low effort, steady yield
  • Ideal for long-term holders
  • Rewards auto-compound on some platforms

Yield Farming

Provide liquidity to DeFi protocols and earn a share of trading fees and platform tokens. APYs can be high but be aware of impermanent loss and smart contract risk.

  • Active DeFi participation
  • Potential for high returns
  • Diversify across platforms

Airdrops

Earn free tokens by participating in new projects, holding certain assets, or completing simple tasks. Some airdrops are worth thousands!

  • Zero upfront cost
  • Follow legit projects
  • Stay alert for scams

Play-to-Earn

Earn crypto and NFTs by playing blockchain games. Rewards depend on skill, activity, and in-game economies.

  • Fun + profit potential
  • Variety of games

Masternodes

  • Higher rewards than basic staking
  • Long-term commitment

No investment is 100% safe. Focus on reputable platforms, diversify, and only use funds you can afford to lock up or risk. Always research before engaging.
